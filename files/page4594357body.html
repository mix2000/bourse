- ABOUT US -

Bourse Architects is the bureau that is devoted to design and establishment of unique facilities from exclusive penthouses and high-end apartments to mountain chalet and sea view villas.

We create an object from scratch. Our clients entrust us the most important thing – that is, their future house. We are the only bureau that undertakes not only design and completion of objects, but also professional selection of the best plots of land or apartments' layout for the best result.

BA is a partner of various global brands, such as Fendi, Brabbu, Jacuzzi etc., that are famous for their interior solutions. We excel at expert level of projects management – we supply and construct objects on our own.
