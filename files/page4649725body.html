- BEDROOM HILLTOP MANSION -
Everlasting desire to discover something new about the life combined with a great will to enhance all its beauty builds a creative mind. A creative mind can be compared with a treasured wonder.
- ABOUT PROJECT -
Creative process is to explore a question that has never been discussed before. Bringing up an idiosyncratic (particular) question you will get a unique answer as well.

Bedroom is a part of fashionable country house in the Andalucía mountain area of southern Spain. Located in the broad area of a hill, this fabulous villa demonstrates admirable ocean views in front and mountains to the left.

The client's request was a master bedroom, dark and luxurious, but at the same time calm and rejuvenating. Taking into account all wishes Taylor Interiors mixed glamorous atmosphere and bespoke Smania products with the usage of qualitative materials such as the freestanding headboard of crocodile leather with a chrome frame.

The palette is consist of chocolate and black-and-grey colours finished by turquoise splashes found in the carefully selected Sabine Abfalterer's triptych artwork.

As well as being sensual the bedroom needed to be technically equipped and contains wardrobes and storage and also motorized blinds that do not distract from the general atmosphere.

The stunning ceiling is a modern representation of original vaulted ceiling and is a weightless cover. The frame is decorated with an ivory hue and the central element is wenge painted oak paneling which gives the room uniqueness and style.
