- ABOUT PROJECT -
The bedroom is part of an exclusive villa in the Andalucía mountain area of southern Spain. Set on expansive grounds at the top of a hill, the front of this stunning property is exposed to striking ocean views, while to the left the vista is mountainous.

Bedroom is a part of fashionable country house in the Andalucía mountain area of southern Spain. Located in the broad area of a hill, this fabulous villa demonstrates admirable ocean views in front and mountains to the left.

The client requested a master bedroom that was dark and sumptuous, yet calm and rejuvenating. Taylor Interiors interpreted these desires by intimately blending glamorous furnishings with bespoke Smania products using the finest materials, which were all carefully sourced, such as the freestanding crocodile leather headboard with a chrome frame.

The client's request was a master bedroom, dark and luxurious, but at the same time calm and rejuvenating. Taking into account all wishes Taylor Interiors mixed glamorous atmosphere and bespoke Smania products with the usage of qualitative materials such as the freestanding headboard of crocodile leather with a chrome frame.

The decadent chocolate black and grey palette is brightened with splashes of turquoise found in the meticulously selected triptych artwork by Sabine Abfalterer.

The palette is consist of chocolate and black-and-grey colours finished by turquoise splashes found in the carefully selected Sabine Abfalterer's triptych artwork.

Though enticingly sensual, the bedroom also needed to be practical and is completed with motorized blinds, wardrobes and storage that do not deflect from the overall ambience.
As well as being sensual the bedroom needed to be technically equipped and contains wardrobes and storage and also motorized blinds that do not distract from the general atmosphere.


The striking ceiling is a modern abstraction of a traditional vaulted ceiling and is a weightless cover. The frame is finished in an ivory hue and the centrepiece is oak panelling stained to wenge, which completes the room with a defining originality and style.

The stunning ceiling is a modern representation of original vaulted ceiling and is a weightless cover. The frame is decorated with an ivory hue and the central element is wenge painted oak paneling which gives the room uniqueness and style.
